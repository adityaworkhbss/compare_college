        Factors to Consider Before College Selection

        A college isn’t just about academics—consider these essentials before selecting.

        Academic Quality

        Check if the college offers a well-structured curriculum and experienced faculty for quality learning.

        Accreditation

        Verify if the college is accredited by bodies like UGC, AICTE, or AIU for trusted education.

        Costs and Financial Aid

        Check if the college offers scholarships, grants, or loans to make your education more affordable.

        Alumni Network

        Ensure the college has a robust alumni network and corporate ties for professional success.

        College Facilities

        Look for modern infrastructure such as libraries, labs, and tools that enhance learning and skill development.

        Placement Support

        Choose a college with strong placement records and industry tie-ups for better career prospects
